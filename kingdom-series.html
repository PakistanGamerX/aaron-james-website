Kingdom Builder Series

The Kingdom Builder series tackles society's deepest fractures with biblical solutions. This groundbreaking 10-book collection addresses anger, poverty, racism, injustice, addiction, loneliness, divorce, bullying, climate care, and migration - not as isolated issues, but as interconnected areas where God's kingdom can break through.

Each title transforms a societal wound into an acronym of hope, showing how Christians can be agents of redemption. From healing personal anger (H.A.T.E) to rebuilding broken relationships (D.I.V.O.R.C.E) to fostering inclusion (M.I.G.R.A.T.I.O.N), this series provides practical frameworks for embodying Christ's love in a fractured world.
